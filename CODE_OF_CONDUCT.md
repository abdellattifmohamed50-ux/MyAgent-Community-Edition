# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

MyAgent contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to make participation
in the project respectful, safe, and welcoming, regardless of experience,
identity, background, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, patient, and technically honest.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Respect privacy, security reports, and confidential disclosures.
- Accept responsibility, correct mistakes, and help others learn.
- Prefer evidence and reproducible examples over authority or hostility.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, doxxing, deliberate
misinformation, security exploitation, and sustained disruption are not
accepted in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository maintainers or the
same private channel used for security reports. Maintainers may remove content,
restrict participation, or ban contributors when necessary. Good-faith reports
will be handled discreetly and without retaliation.

This policy applies to repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, community
channels, events, and public representation of the project.
